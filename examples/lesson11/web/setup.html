To setup the authentication realm and create users follow these instructions:

Setting up the user groups.

  • Login into weblogic admin console
  • Use login: "weblogic" and password "welcome1"
  • Click on the "Security Realms" link on the left sidebar.
  • Click on the "myrealm" Realm in the table.
  • Click on the "Users & Groups" tab.
  • Click the "Groups" sub-tab.
  • create the following groups if they do not exist by clicking the "New" button.
Group Description
quizAdmin Administrator, can delete and create quizzes.
quizUser A registered user may answer quizzes.
quizInstructor An instructor may review scores.

Use DefaultAuthenticator as the Provider for all of them.

Creating users.

  • Now click on the Users sub-tab.
  • Click on the "New" button
  • Set the name: quizAdministrator
  • Provider: DefaultAuthenticator
  • Password: quiz123123
  • Confirm the password: quiz123123
  • Click "OK"
  • Click on the newly created user: quizAdministrator
  • Click on the "groups" tab.
  • Select quizAdmin from the list on the left and click the single blue right arrow.
  • Click the "Save" button.
  • On the top "navigation crumb bar" click on Users and Groups to go back to the list of users
